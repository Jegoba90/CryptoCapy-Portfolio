# Data Attribution — CryptoCapi

CryptoCapi aggregates data from the following third-party providers. Each provider's data is subject to their own terms of service.

---

## Market Data

**CoinGecko** — Primary source for cryptocurrency prices, market capitalization, trading volume, global market metrics, and exchange data.
[coingecko.com](https://www.coingecko.com)

**Binance** — Live OHLCV candle data used exclusively by the Quant Pro engine for on-demand dual-timeframe analysis.
[binance.com](https://www.binance.com)

**Yahoo Finance** — Historical daily price data (90-day window) used for statistical calculations in the Quant Plus and Radar engines.

---

## Macroeconomic Data

**FRED (Federal Reserve Economic Data)** — Macroeconomic indicators including Federal Funds Rate, CPI, DXY, M2 money supply, and 10-Year Treasury yield. Provided by the Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis.
[fred.stlouisfed.org](https://fred.stlouisfed.org)

**World Bank Open Data** — Annual CPI change by country, used for the Global Inflation endpoint.
[data.worldbank.org](https://data.worldbank.org)

---

## News & Content

Radar engine insights are informed by headlines and article content from the following sources via their public RSS feeds. Original content belongs to its respective authors and publishers.

- [Cointelegraph](https://cointelegraph.com)
- [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com)
- [Decrypt](https://decrypt.co)
- [The Block](https://www.theblock.co)
- [Bitcoin Magazine](https://bitcoinmagazine.com)
- [CryptoSlate](https://cryptoslate.com)
- [CryptoPotato](https://cryptopotato.com)
- [Bitcoinist](https://bitcoinist.com)

CryptoCapi accesses public RSS feeds only. Original article content is not stored — it is processed to extract relevant signals and then discarded.

[mempool.space](https://mempool.space)

[alchemy.com](https://www.alchemy.com)
